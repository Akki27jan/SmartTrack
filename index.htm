How it works

Five steps from hiring doubt
to verified certainty

01
Company Registers
Verify GSTIN, set up your HR team with role-based access and MFA on every account.
02
Upload Exit Records
HR submits records at exit — salary verified via OCR payslip, conduct flags backed by evidence.
03
Employee Reviews
Every employee is notified, gets 15 days to review, dispute, or add a personal statement.
04
You Search in 2s
Enter PAN + name. Get the full verified employment history in under 2 seconds. No calls needed.
05
Download & Decide
Download a watermarked PDF report, legally valid only for your company. Audit trail included.
